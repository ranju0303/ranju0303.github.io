Product

nuclearSense offers a comprehensive IoT platform that seamlessly integrates hardware and software to monitor, analyze, and visualize data from critical assets. Our solution combines real-time data acquisition, cloud-based analytics, and intuitive dashboards to enable predictive maintenance and informed decision-making.

  • Real-time Monitoring: Track sensor data continuously with instant alerts for anomalies.
  • Predictive Analytics: Utilize machine learning to predict issues and schedule maintenance proactively.
  • Seamless Integration: Connect effortlessly with third-party systems like ThingsBoard, Dewesoft, and Petasense.
  • Scalable & Secure: Benefit from a cloud architecture that grows with your needs while keeping data protected.

Our platform is built to work hand-in-hand with industry-leading tools and sensors:

  • ThingsBoard: An open-source IoT platform integrated for robust device management and data visualization.
  • Dewesoft: High-performance data acquisition systems ensure accurate and high-speed data collection from sensors.
  • Petasense: Wireless vibration sensors feed critical equipment health data into nuclearSense for predictive maintenance.

Architecture

The nuclearSense architecture is designed for flexibility and scalability, allowing seamless integration across the entire data pipeline:

Sensors & Devices

Connect a wide range of sensors (e.g., Petasense vibration sensors) and devices capturing critical data at the edge.

Data Acquisition

Aggregate and preprocess data in real-time using Dewesoft and IoT gateways for reliable, high-speed data collection.

Cloud Platform

Stream data to the cloud via ThingsBoard, enabling scalable storage, processing, and device management with robust security.

Dashboard & Alerts

Visualize insights on interactive dashboards and receive instant alerts anywhere, through web or mobile interfaces.
